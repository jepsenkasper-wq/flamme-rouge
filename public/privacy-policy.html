Flamme Rouge: Grand Tour Companion Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Version: 1.0
Last updated: July 2026

Flamme Rouge: Grand Tour Companion is an unofficial companion app for the Flamme Rouge Grand Tour board game.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app stores, how it is used, and when it may be shared.

Information stored

The app stores only the information you choose to enter, including:

  • Game names
  • Player names
  • Stage results
  • Classifications
  • Tour Points
  • Follow Codes for shared games

This information is used solely to manage your Grand Tour campaigns.

Local storage

By default, all games are stored locally on your own device using local storage.

No user account is required to use the app.

Shared games

If you choose to share a game using a Follow Code, the necessary game information is securely synchronized through our online service (Supabase) so that other players can follow your Grand Tour in real time.

Only the information required for synchronization is uploaded.

Personal information

The app does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Payment information

unless you voluntarily enter such information as part of a player name or game name.

Analytics and advertising

Flamme Rouge: Grand Tour Companion:

  • Does not display advertisements.
  • Does not use analytics services.
  • Does not use tracking technologies.
  • Does not collect marketing data.

Third-party services

The app uses Supabase solely to synchronize shared games between players.

No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

Data security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect shared game data during transmission and storage.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your control

You remain in control of your data.

You can:

  • Delete local games at any time.
  • Stop sharing a game whenever you choose.
  • Remove followed games from your device.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for players of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if new functionality is added to the app.

The latest version will always be made available online.
